Driving Directions to La Refoulerie, Lassay-les-Chateaux

 

Calpe is about 65 miles north of Alicante and 80 miles south of Valencia. Most flights come into Alicante, but it is also possible to get flights to Valencia. You can hire a car at either Airport.The easiest route from the airport is via the A7 toll motorway (less than €10). If you are not hiring a car then you can get a taxi transfer from Alicante Airport with Alicante Taxi Transfers.

 

Directions from Calais to Lassay Les Chateaux (approx. 4 to 5 hours)

 

The best way is via toll motorway (approx. €20). From Calais follow the signs to Paris and then Boulogne on the Autoroute A16. Remain on the A16 to Abbeville. On approaching Abbeville take the A28 towards Rouen. Before you get to Rouen take the A29 (A28, J11) to Le Harve. After passing over two very spectacular toll bridges continue on the A29 to the south of Le Havre. At the end of the A29 take the A13 to Caen. Take the Caen ring road (periferique) and follow signs to Alencon and Le Mans (J13 Porte d’Espagne). Continue from Caen to Lassay Les Chateaux below.

 

Directions from Caen to Lassay Les Chateaux (approx. 1 hour)

 

From the port of Ouistreham follow signs to Caen and the A13 on the D514. Take the Caen ring road (periferique est) following signs to Alencon and Le Mans (J13 Porte d’Espagne). Join the N158 sign posted to Alencon, Le Mans and Falaise (J13 of the periferique) . This is a fast dual carriageway. After approximately 20 miles on the N158 near Falaise take the D909 to Bagnoles de l'Orne. Continue on the D909 until you come to Fromentel and take the D19 to Bagnoles de l'Orne and La Ferte-Mace. When you arrive at La Ferte-Mace take the D916 to Bagnoles de L'Orne (watch carefully for the signs to Bagnoles). Continue on the D916 bypassing Bagnoles and following signs to Lassay, Mayenne and Alencon. Remain on the D916 until you arrive at Couterne. Continue straight on to Lassay on the D34. When you arrive at Lassay you have the choice of continuing through the town or going via the bypass, see below.

 

Directions from Lassay-les-Chateaux to La Refoulerie

 

Option 1 - bypass Lassay:

 

Just before you get to Lassay you will come to a roundabout approximately 6 miles after passing through Couterne. At this roundabout take the first exit left to Mayenne to bypass Lassay-les-Chateaux. At the next roundabout continue straight on, again to Mayenne. At the next roundabout take the first exit left back on to the D34 to Mayenne and Le Hopes. Continue From Roundabout below.

 

Option 2 - Drive through Lassay:

 

As you enter Lassay follow the signs to Mayenne. You will turn left at the first junction in Lassay and then the second right to Mayenne on the D34. Shortly after leaving Lassay on the D34 you will come to a roundabout. Continue From Roundabout below.

From Roundabout:

About 400 yards from the roundabout on the D34 look out for the signpost to Couberie and Le Horps to the left at a traffic island. Turn left here and drive through the village of Couberie (just a few houses) past the church on the LHS. Just as you are leaving the village you will see a small road/driveway on the right and a very small sign to La Refoulerie. Drive to the end of this road/driveway and you will see the cottage on the right hand side. You can drive over the grass into the property. If there has been a lot of rain please take care that you do not get stuck in the mud. You may prefer to leave your car at the end of the driveway. Please do not park on the grass.
