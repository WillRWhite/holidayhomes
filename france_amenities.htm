Amenities







 
 

In the local town square of Lassay next to the Mairie is a 8 à Hui where you can get most essential items. There is also a main supermarket, Intermarche at the first roundabout on the main road (D34) into Lassay from Bagnoles All shops are closed on French Bank Holidays and at 12:00am on Sunday. In Lassay, however you will always find one bread shop (either Aux Délices de Lassay or Boulangerie & Patisserie) open every day of the week between 7:00am and 7:00pm. Most shops are  also closed for Lunch normally between 12:30 and 14:30. Banks are usually shut on Mondays but are open half day on Saturday.

In an emergency there is a small shop called Le Relais des Mousquetaires, Tel : 09 62 38 81 61 which sells essential items approximately 15Km away in the small village of Loré. It open every day apart from Tuesday form 9:00 to 19:30 ad closed for lunch from 13:00 to 14:30, 15:00 on Sunday. See the area map.

There is no refuse collection service in Lassay. Please leave you waste, well sealed in the large green bin outside the house. Recycling can de deposed of  in the  recycling and refuse disposal areas on the way into Lassay from the house just on the right before you enter the town. There is even a main recycling centre near the Intermarche supermarket.

Lassay-les-Chateaux is the nearest village with all amenities - General store, 3 Boulangerie / Patisserie (bakers / cake shop), Butcher, 2 Banks, Post Office, 2 Florists, Pharmacy, Optician, Newsagent etc. Also there is a good sized supermarket (Intermarche) just outside of the town to the north.

Bagnoles de l’Orne, approximately 10 miles north, is a lovely Spa town with lots of leisure facilities, restaurants, hotels, Casino and a lake. Ferte Mace is approximately 15 miles north with a very large hypermarket - E.Leclerc and again shops, bars and restaurants.
