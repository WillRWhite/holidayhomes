Right in the centre of the Costa Blanca, El Alamo is situated just outside the old fishing village of Calpe, which is now a vibrant town with plenty of bars and restaurants. In easy reach of either Alicante or Valencia airport. There is a spectacular sandy blue flag beach (Playa La Fossa / Levante) approximately 10 minutes walking distance. The town of Calpe with another lovely beach is about 5 minutes in the car or if you are feeling fit you can walk along the seafront. There are lots of other things to do including many close golf clubs and If you fancy a "wild" night out, then Benidorm is less than 45 minutes drive.

The apartment is situated on the second floor and is comfortably furnished.

 

 

 

 

El Alamo is in an up and coming area just outside Calpe and the local supermarket is just outside the front door.
