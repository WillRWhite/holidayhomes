Post Booking Instructions for El Alamo, France

 
 

On Arrival
If you have not already got the keys, you will normally find them under a flower pot outside the front door.

Please contact our local agent, Jan or Brian Lane to let them know you have arrived and confirm date and approximate time you will be leaving to exchange keys and arrange house clean.

Tel:    +33 (0)2 43 30 46 80
Email: janlane@wanadoo.fr

Our neighbours are a very nice couple with 2 young children. Please do say hello if you see them. It is very useful to know them in case of immediate problems they will be able to help you.

Dustbins
There are no dustbins collections. Please take all of your rubbish to one of the many refuse points. The nearest in in Couberie on the only road to the left as you approach from La Refoulerie.

Telephone
The telephone number at La Refoulerie is: +33 (0)2 43 00 37 78. Please note this only allows incoming calls but you can use it to dial emergency numbers as below. You can also purchase a telephone cards to make outgoing calls from most tabacs.

Emergencies
Emergency dial 18 (this covers medical, fire and all emergencies).
Operator dial 12.

Doctor
Dr. Le Croix can be found on the RHS just before you turn left at the junction going into Lassay - it is a house and you may see an ambulance outside. Telephone
