El Alamo, Calpe (Alicante), Spain. Sleeps 4 to 6
 
 

Holiday Homes in France & Spain

        

Amenities







 
 

Five to ten minutes walk to a golden sandy beach, with lots of good reasonably priced restaurants along the sea front. There is a more up market restaurant for that special occasion only 5 minutes walk away El Alamo (Turn right outside El Alamo and La Viña de Calpe is less than five minutes walk on the right. Booking is advisable, Tel: 96 583 09 55).

Next door to El Alamo is a large supermarket, where all provisions can be purchased.

The charming old Calpe town is a pleasant 30 to 40 minute walk along the sea front or a five minute car ride. Taxis and a local bus service are also available into town. Here you will find more traditional style restaurants and also a mixture of Italian, Chinese and Indian and British cafes, not to mention good quality shoe shops and fashionable boutiques for both sexes.

Apart from all the pretty coastal resorts scattered along this Eastern coastline other local attractions include a bird sanctuary on the Penon de Ifach and Flamingos on a Salt Lake, just minutes walk from the apartment. If you hire a car you can reach other resorts such as Moraira (delightful resort) following the main windy road just outside El Alamo.
