Things To Do - Golf







Golf

The Costa Blanca is without doubt a golf players paradise. There are over fifteen 18 hole golf courses in the Costa Blanca, all within a 2 hour drive of Calpe and most less than 1 hour. Some have been designed by Seve Ballesteros, some regularly play host to Spanish national championships, some are even ranked regularly amongst the top courses in the world.

There are a variety of styles and terrains to choose; from stunning coastline to rugged mountains and levels of difficulty to cater for all standards. Tuition and golf academies will help you fine-tune your swing, whilst plenty of driving ranges and practice greens will build up your confidence before you even set foot on the course.
