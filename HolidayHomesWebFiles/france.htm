La Refoulerie, a charming 200 year old two bedroom cottage just on the boarder of Normandy and Pays de la Loire, less than two hours drive from the port of Caen. Located in a tiny hamlet called Couberie just 1 mile outside the beautiful town of Lassay-les-Chateaux, where you will find local shops, a large supermarket, cafes and restaurants. The Spa town of Bagnoles de l'Orne is just 20 minutes drive, with many activities available for all the family. During the day there is a choice of open air heated swimming pool, tennis courts, horse riding and acres of forests to discover by bike. At night you will find a variety of good quality restaurants, bars, hotels, cinema and a casino for a little flutter.

La Refoulerie is surrounded by 2.5 acres of private gardens, fields and woods and has a small stream running through it.

 

 

 

 

The castle in Lassay-les-Chateaux is just 5 minutes drive from La Refoulerie and although privately owned the castle and grounds are open to the public. During the summer months guided tours occur at regular intervals during the day.
